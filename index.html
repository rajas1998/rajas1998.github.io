Hi! I am Rajas Bansal, a second year MS in CS student at Stanford, specializing in AI. As an Applied Scientist Intern at Amazon Search, I worked on scaling graph neural on large graphs through task based sampling. At Stanford, I worked with Prof. Jure Leskovec on graph neural networks and recommender systems. In the past, I worked as a Research Engineer at Cohesity building distributed systems for large scale data.
I am interested in applying ML research to solve impactful real world problems. If you want to chat about my work or interesting project, feel free to reach out to me.
